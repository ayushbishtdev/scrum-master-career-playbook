# Scrum Master ATS & Interview Cheatsheet

## 1. ATS Keyword Optimization

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) reject resumes lacking measurable impact. Use these terms to secure callbacks.

**High-Value Keywords (Include these):**
* Cycle Time Reduction
* Impediment Resolution
* Cross-Functional Leadership
* Framework specifics (SAFe, LeSS, Kanban)
* Predictive Planning

**Resume Kill Words (Delete these immediately):**
* Servant leader
* Team player
* Managed ceremonies / Scheduled meetings

**Impact Formatting Formula:**
*Poor:* "Facilitated daily stand-ups."
*Strong:* "Reduced average sprint cycle time by 15% over three months by identifying and eliminating cross-departmental bottlenecks."

## 2. Interview Prep: The STAR Method

Interviewers test your behavioral responses to friction, not your ability to quote the Scrum Guide.

**Question:** "Tell me about a time you handled a conflict."
* **Situation:** Business demands aggressively clashed with development capacity.
* **Task:** Needed to align stakeholders without breaking the sprint.
* **Action:** Facilitated a data-driven (not emotional) conversation using velocity metrics to visualize capacity constraints.
* **Result:** Reached a mutually beneficial compromise, protecting the team's sustainable pace while delivering the critical business feature.

**Question:** "Will AI replace your role?"
* **Answer Strategy:** Do not dismiss the threat. Acknowledge that AI replaces basic administrative tracking. Position yourself as an **AI Orchestrator** who leverages these tools to automate metrics and dramatically increase team velocity, making you an irreplaceable asset.
