# Agile Career Pivot Checklist

## Pivot 1: Traditional Project Manager ➡️ Scrum Master

This transition is common but frequently botched due to residual command-and-control habits.

* [ ] **Unlearn Task Assignment:** Completely abandon managing the "how" and "when" of technical tasks; the team must self-organize.
* [ ] **Repurpose Skills:** Pivot your existing Risk Mitigation skills to identify systemic blockers before they derail sprints.
* [ ] **Repurpose Skills:** Pivot your Stakeholder Management skills to shield developers from external scope creep.
* [ ] **Acquire Certifications:** A PMP proves large-scale risk comprehension, but you *must* add a PSM I or CSM to pass ATS resume screenings.

## Pivot 2: Scrum Master ➡️ Product Owner

This is a lateral pivot to a completely different discipline, requiring a shift from process to product.

* [ ] **Shift Focus:** Stop optimizing the internal delivery system and start obsessing over market dynamics and customer empathy.
* [ ] **Master Negotiation:** Develop the ability to confidently say "no" to executives to protect backlog integrity.
* [ ] **Develop Financial Acumen:** Learn to manage return on investment (ROI), revenue forecasting, and product profitability.
* [ ] **Avoid the Dual-Role Trap:** Never accept a job asking you to be both SM and PO simultaneously. It creates an inescapable conflict of interest between delivery demands and sustainable pace.
* [ ] **Acquire Certifications:** Secure a PSPO or CSPO to validate foundational value delivery knowledge.
